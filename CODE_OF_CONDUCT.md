# Community code of conduct

## Our standard

Participation in this repository must remain respectful, evidence-based and
focused on improving the public research record. Examples of expected behavior
include:

- Cite first-party or reproducible evidence for factual corrections.
- State uncertainty and respect each dataset's published limitations.
- Critique methods and claims without attacking contributors or named people.
- Protect private faces, account information, credentials and unpublished personal data.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, deliberate misinformation,
doxxing, non-consensual identity media, sexualized content involving real
people, spam and attempts to expose private user data are unacceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This standard applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, release comments
and other public interaction associated with the repository. Maintainers may
edit or remove content, close contributions, restrict participation or report
serious abuse when needed to protect contributors and users.

Report conduct concerns privately to <support@faceswapai.com> with the subject
`Open research conduct report`. Include links and the minimum evidence needed
for review; do not repost harmful or private material in a public issue.

Enforcement decisions will consider context, severity, recurrence, user safety
and the integrity of the public evidence record.
