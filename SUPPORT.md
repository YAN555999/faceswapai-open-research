# Support

Use the channel that matches the request:

- Research data correction: open a public data-correction issue with the file, field, first-party evidence URL and proposed correction.
- Reproducibility question: open an issue without attaching private faces, customer uploads or account data.
- Security vulnerability: follow [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) and report it privately.
- FaceSwapAI product, account, generation or billing support: email <support@faceswapai.com>.
- Editorial or media request: use <https://faceswapai.com/press>.

This repository does not provide account support and must not be used to share
credentials, payment details, private identity media or unpublished personal
information.
